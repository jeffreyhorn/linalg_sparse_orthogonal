Related comparison: Day 7 vs Day 8 spectral measurements
----------------------------------------------------------

  Day-to-day variance on the spectral path's nnz_L on regular-
  structured fixtures is small (≤ 0.5 % typical).  Kuu shows
  wider variance day-to-day:
    Day 7 spectral: Kuu = 907 913 (-1.8 % vs default)
    Day 8 spectral: Kuu = 708 650 (-23.3 % vs default)

  Hypothesis: Kuu's irregular SPD structure produces a
  Pres_Poisson-class Fiedler vector with several clustered
  eigenvalues near λ_1.  The Lanczos backend's grow-m strategy
  may converge to slightly different Ritz pairs across runs
  (per the Sprint 20 tol/reorthogonalize discussion).  Day 7
  + Day 8 results both fall within the "spectral is a notable
  Kuu win" band, but the magnitude is sensitive.  Day 9's
  sweep should re-measure with multiple runs per setting to
  bound the Kuu variance.

  3's HCC analysis for the analogous mechanism).
